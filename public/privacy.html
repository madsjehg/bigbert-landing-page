Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

"i'm a stick figure. i don't know what to do with your data. but legally we have to tell you what happens with it. so here we go." - bert

1. What We Collect

When you use bigbert, we collect the following information that you provide during onboarding:

  • Gender, age, height, and weight
  • Fitness goals and experience level
  • Workout preferences, schedule, and equipment access
  • Injury information you choose to share

This information is used solely to generate your personalized workout plan. It is stored locally on your device and sent to our server only when generating or regenerating your plan.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your onboarding data is sent to our secure API to generate a personalized workout plan using AI (Claude by Anthropic). The data is:

  • Used only for workout plan generation
  • Not stored on our servers after the plan is generated
  • Not sold, shared, or used for advertising
  • Not used to train AI models

3. Data Stored on Your Device

bigbert stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Your onboarding responses
  • Your generated workout plan
  • Workout completion history, XP, level, and streaks
  • Notification preferences

This data never leaves your device except during plan generation.

4. Subscriptions & Payments

Subscriptions are processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information. Subscription management is handled through your Apple ID account.

5. Push Notifications

bigbert sends local push notifications to remind you about workouts. These are scheduled on your device and do not involve any server communication. You can disable them anytime in the app's settings or your device settings.

6. Analytics & Tracking

bigbert does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. We don't track your behavior, location, or app usage patterns.

7. Children's Privacy

bigbert is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Data Deletion

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by uninstalling the app. This permanently removes all locally stored data. If you'd like assistance with data deletion, contact us at hello@bigbert.app.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at hello@bigbert.app.
